Kevin Missal Book Café

Where books, coffee, and community come together.

A Reader's Retreat

Imagine the aroma of freshly brewed coffee mingling with the scent of new books, shelves lined with timeless classics curated by bestselling author Kevin Missal, and cozy nooks where conversations pause so stories can take over. This isn't just a café — it's a cultural space where books, coffee, and community come together.

More Than Just a Menu

From morning lattes to late-evening poetry sessions, the café offers more than just a menu. Sip a cappuccino while flipping through a Pocket Classic, attend a weekend author meet-and-greet, or join our book swap shelf tradition.

We host regular events — readings, workshops, and open mics — making sure that every visit feels different, yet familiar.

The Publishing Backbone

Behind the café lies a publishing powerhouse: through Hubhawks, NuVoice Press, and Solomon Press, we've sold millions of books and built one of India's strongest networks for distribution and literary engagement.

Our shelves don't just display books — they represent our own imprints, box sets, and curated collections, giving every reader access to beautifully produced stories.

Franchise With Us

For those who dream bigger, the Kevin Missal Book Café franchise model extends this vision across cities. With a zero franchise fee, transparent revenue share, and proven publishing-plus-café synergy, we empower partners to run profitable outlets while being part of a brand that readers already trust.

Your Place for Stories

Whether you’re here for a quiet read, a lively discussion, a cup of coffee, or to explore a business opportunity — Kevin Missal Book Café is your place where stories brew, connections spark, and communities grow.
